Who am I?

I am a passionate and driven high school student with a deep interest in electrical engineering, particularly in embedded systems, dual-MCU architectures, and robotics control systems. Throughout my academic journey, I have actively sought opportunities to innovate and lead, earning recognition as an ISEF 2025 participant for my aerospace control systems work.

As team leader of my school's robotics program, I spearheaded the transition to advanced swerve drive systems and pioneered custom simulation tools that have been adopted by teams nationwide. My technical expertise spans from PCB design and microcontroller programming to full-stack web development and aerospace applications.

Beyond technical achievements, I dedicate over 150 hours annually to STEM outreach, teaching elementary and middle school students with a focus on increasing diversity in engineering. My research has earned corporate sponsorships from industry leaders including EasyEDA, Sipeed, and OSHPark, validating the real-world impact of my innovations.

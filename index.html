A driven high school student with a deep focus on electrical engineering, particularly embedded systems, dual-MCU architectures, and robotics control systems. My work earned recognition as an ISEF 2025 participant for aerospace control systems research.

As team leader of my school's robotics program, I spearheaded the transition to advanced swerve drive systems and built custom simulation tools now adopted by teams nationwide. Technical expertise spans from PCB design and microcontroller programming to full-stack development and aerospace applications.

Beyond hardware, I dedicate over 150 hours annually to STEM outreach — teaching elementary and middle school students with a focus on increasing diversity in engineering. Corporate sponsorships from EasyEDA, Sipeed, and OSHPark validate the real-world impact.

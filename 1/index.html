Who am I?

I am a passionate and driven high school student with a deep interest in engineering, particularly in control systems, embedded systems, and avionics. Throughout my academic journey, I have actively sought opportunities to learn, innovate, and lead. As the captain of my school's robotics team, I led the Metrobots to several offseason wins and spearheaded projects like custom robot simulators and team websites, enhancing both performance and strategic abilities.

Beyond robotics, I founded Glimmer, an open-source visual website builder, and have honed my programming skills through diverse projects ranging from web development to custom drones. I also dedicate over 300 hours to STEM outreach, teaching elementary and middle school students, with a focus on increasing diversity in engineering.

My long-term goals are centered around using technology to empower underserved communities, creating low-cost, accessible solutions that address real-world challenges. I am eager to further develop my skills through rigorous academic and research experiences, collaborating with like-minded peers, and continuing my journey to make a meaningful impact in both engineering and education.
